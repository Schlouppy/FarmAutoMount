Short description:
Automatically remounts after gathering herbs, mining ore, or gathering lumber. Won't interfere if you can gather while mounted. Lightweight and simple - just install and forget.

Full description (HTML for CurseForge):

<h2>Farm Auto Mount</h2>

<p>Automatically resummons your mount right after gathering any resource — herbs, ore, or lumber. No more fumbling with keybinds between each node.</p>

<p>If you can gather without dismounting (e.g. mounted lumber harvesting), the addon detects it and stays out of the way.</p>

<p>Written from scratch as a learning project, inspired by AutoMountFix. Built with the help of AI (Claude).</p>

<h3>Features</h3>
<ul>
<li><b>Auto-remount</b> — Mounts automatically after you finish looting a gathering node</li>
<li><b>Mounted gathering</b> — Won't interfere if you can gather without dismounting</li>
<li><b>Custom mount</b> — Choose a specific mount per character or account-wide</li>
<li><b>Configurable delay</b> — Adjust the delay before mounting (default: 0.5s)</li>
<li><b>Druid-friendly</b> — Automatically skips druids (Travel Form is better!)</li>
<li><b>Error notifications</b> — Shows a message when mounting fails (in combat, interrupted, etc. - disabled by default)</li>
<li><b>10 languages</b> — EN, FR, DE, ES, IT, PT, RU, KO, ZH-CN, ZH-TW</li>
</ul>

<h3>Commands</h3>
<table>
<tr><td><b>/fam mount &lt;name&gt;</b></td><td>Set mount for this character</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>/fam account &lt;name&gt;</b></td><td>Set mount for all characters</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>/fam reset</b></td><td>Reset character mount (use account setting)</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>/fam reset account</b></td><td>Reset account mount (use favourite)</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>/fam enable / disable</b></td><td>Toggle the addon</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>/fam delay &lt;seconds&gt;</b></td><td>Adjust the remount delay</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>/fam silent</b></td><td>Toggle error notifications</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>/fam debug</b></td><td>Toggle debug mode</td></tr>
</table>

<p><b>Tip:</b> Shift+click a mount in your journal to paste its name into chat!</p>

<h3>FAQ</h3>
<p><b>I can gather lumber while mounted, will the addon cause issues?</b><br/>
No! The addon detects if you're already mounted and won't try to remount.</p>

<p><b>Why doesn't it work for my Druid?</b><br/>
Druids are automatically skipped. Travel Form is instant and better than any mount.</p>

<p><b>I don't want error messages in chat</b><br/>
Error notifications are silent by default since v1.1.5. Use <code>/fam silent</code> to toggle them on.</p>
